About the Artist

Dcn. Romanos is an Orthodox Deacon with a profound love for Byzantine Iconography. With over a decade of carving experience, he combines his devotion to liturgical arts with his passion for craftsmanship.

His journey was enriched by studying under master carver Jonathan Pageau, where he mastered the fundamentals of icon carving. Today, he continues to deepen his understanding and appreciation of Byzantine iconography in all its forms.

Dcn. Romanos creates commissioned pieces for churches and individuals, specializing in:

  • Carved Icons
  • Prosphora Stamps
  • Liturgical Crosses
  • Custom Religious Art
