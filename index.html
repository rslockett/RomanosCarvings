About the Artist

Crafting Sacred Art with Devotion

Dcn. Romanos is an Orthodox Deacon whose ministry extends into the realm of sacred art through Byzantine iconography and liturgical woodcarving. With over a decade of experience, he combines traditional techniques with deep theological understanding to create pieces that serve both liturgical function and spiritual contemplation.

His journey in this sacred art form was enriched through study under master carver Jonathan Pageau, where he developed a profound understanding of the symbolic language and technical precision required in Orthodox iconography.

Areas of Expertise

  • Hand-Carved Icons
  • Prosphora Stamps
  • Liturgical Crosses
  • Custom Religious Art

Commission Information
